Our Support & Contact

We provide 24/7 support service to all members of Maria Barbara Central Library.
The Hotline can be accessed via the nationwide number 1−800−094. The Hotline provides service referrals to agencies in all 21 states and Emma Stone Islands.
Services are provided without regard to race, color, national origin, religion, gender, age, or disability (including deaf and hard of hearing).
